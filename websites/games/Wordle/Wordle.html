How to Play Wordle

🎯 Objective

Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 attempts or fewer!

📝 How to Play

• Select a word category from the dropdown menu

• Type your guess using the on-screen keyboard or your physical keyboard

• Press ENTER to submit your guess

• Use BACKSPACE to delete letters

• Any valid 5-letter English word is accepted for guesses

• The target word will be from your selected category

📚 Categories

Choose from dozens of word categories like Science, Sports, Food, Geography, and more! The target word will be from your selected category, but you can guess any valid English word.

💡 Tips

• Start with words that contain common vowels and consonants

• Pay attention to the keyboard colors - they show which letters you've tried

• Use your yellow letters in different positions in subsequent guesses

• Don't repeat gray letters in future guesses

• Try different categories for varied difficulty levels

• Think about the category theme when making your guesses

🏆 Scoring

You win when you guess the word correctly! Try to solve it in as few guesses as possible.
